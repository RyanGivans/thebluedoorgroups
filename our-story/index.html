Our Story

Built through experience. Strengthened through change.

The Blue Door Group story began with modest projects, practical lessons, and a belief that long-term growth comes from learning, adapting, and owning the assets that shape the future.

Kurt Carlson

From treehouses to a vertically integrated real estate company.

Kurt Carlson first began designing and building custom treehouses in the 1990s. The early projects were modest, but they created an important testing ground for materials, construction methods, and supplier relationships.

That hands-on experience became the foundation for a broader strategy: acquire assets, create value through thoughtful development, build reliable cash flow, and use accumulated equity to support the next opportunity.

The Journey

A story of continuous learning and deliberate growth.

Each chapter added a new layer of experience, resilience, and opportunity to the Blue Door model.

1990s

The Beginning

Custom treehouses became an early testing ground for building materials, practical construction techniques, and supplier relationships.

Early 2000s

Steady Growth

Growth remained measured while hands-on projects strengthened the knowledge that would later support a larger development strategy.

2007–2009

Lessons From the Housing Downturn

During the market downturn, Kurt studied the relationship between banking institutions, housing, and asset ownership while continuing to build practical experience through remodeling partnerships.

Ownership Mindset

A Defining Realization

Mentorship and real-world experience revealed a core principle that continues to guide Blue Door today: durable growth in real estate requires asset ownership.

Strategic Land Investment

Creating the Next Opportunity

The company began buying and selling land parcels, creating a pathway toward internally financed residential development and stronger long-term returns.

2021

The Blue Door Properties

Rental acquisitions added dependable monthly cash flow, portfolio stability, and accumulated equity. Today, The Blue Door Properties operates 31 rental properties.

2025

The Blue Door Investment

After proving the private-banking model through successful internal projects, Blue Door established a dedicated investment division focused on strategic capital and larger opportunities.

Today

The Next Chapter

Kurt now works as a retail consultant and full-time real estate developer, dedicating his energy to thoughtful growth, regional expansion, and collaborative partnerships.

The Blue Door Philosophy

Old ways don’t open new doors.

The story is far from over. It is simply the beginning of a new chapter built on continuous learning, disciplined ownership, and the right partnerships.
